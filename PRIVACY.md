# Privacy Policy for Fenstr

**Last updated: April 22, 2026**

## Overview

Fenstr is a window management utility for Windows. Your privacy is important to us, and Fenstr is designed with privacy in mind.

## Data Collection

Fenstr does **not** collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Specifically:

- No telemetry or analytics data is collected
- No usage data is tracked
- No network connections are made
- No data is shared with third parties

## Local Data

Fenstr stores your configuration preferences (such as hotkey bindings and monitor division settings) locally on your device at `%AppData%\Fenstr\config.json`. This data never leaves your device.

## Windows API Access

Fenstr requires full trust access to interact with the Windows desktop environment (window positioning, mouse and keyboard hooks). These capabilities are used solely for window management functionality and do not involve accessing personal files or data.

## Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on the [GitHub repository](https://github.com/patrickiel/Fenstr).
